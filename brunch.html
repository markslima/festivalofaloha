Forming a New Hawaiian Civic Club

Presenter: C. Lanialoha Lee-Sumberg and Oliver J. Larson, Esq., guest speakers

Sponsor: Hawai`i Maoli and the Office of Hawaiian Affairs

THURSDAY, MAY 24, 11:00am -1:00pm — GALLERY ROOM, WEST BUILDING — $5


Native Hawaiians and other Pacific Islanders interested in forming a Hawaiian civic club in their communities should attend this important workshop. The goal of the presentation is to provide the tools and instruction necessary to complete the application process. Learn about the Hawaiian Civic Club movement, how the convention process works, and the nuts and bolts of forming a new club. Mr. Larson will be available to answer any questions regarding establishing legal entities.


The New Hawaiian Civic Club meeting will be held at the Old Town School of Folk Music in the Gallery Room, West Building. A $5 cash-only fee will be collected on site.

Native Hawaiian and other Asian and Pacific Islanders Health Awareness Brunch

Presenters: Ke Ali`i Victoria Ka`iulani Hawaiian Civic Club, Kōmike Mālama Ola Officers: Dr. Lareina Pedriquez

and Dr. Robert Richart. Guest Speakers

Sponsor: The Office of Hawaiian Affairs

FRIDAY, MAY 24, 11:00am-1:00pm — LOCATION TBA


Representing the Ke Ali`i Victoria Ka`iulani Hawaiian Civic Club of Chicago, and the Association of Hawaiian Civic Clubs, Kōmike Mālama Ola (Health Committee) and to partner with other health providers, our goal is to promote health education, early detection screening, and change to healthier lifestyles to reduce the native Hawaiian and other Asian and Pacific Islander’s health disparities. Please join us for breakfast, fellowship, and sharing of stories that can inspire us to enjoy life longer with our family and friends.
