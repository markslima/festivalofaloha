KVKHCC Community Engagement Project with support from the Office of Hawaiian Affairs — Native Hawaiian Community Assessment Survey


Tell us about yourself.


The Office of Hawaiian Affairs (OHA) is sponsoring the Community Assessment Survey. The goal is to develop a profile of Native Hawaiian communities across the continental US and the state of Hawai`i. This information will be used to improve the conditions of Native Hawaiians wherever they live. Your responses are extremely valuable and help OHA better understand our communities. A small time investment on your part now will help guide strategic priorities for years to come. Your feedback is important – don’t miss this chance to be heard.

Kūpa'a Questionnaire, for Hawaiians and Hawaiians-at-heart


We want to get to know you.


Please take a minute to fill out the survey. Our goal is to identify Pacific Islanders as well as friends and families of Aloha residing in the Midwest. By getting to know you a little better we can tailor our educational and cultural programs to meet your needs. We don’t share this information with anyone else and your responses will be confidential. Mahalo for your kokua!
