We are excited to present Chicago's first Festival of Aloha. It is four days of pono workshops and four nights of concerts dedicated to the traditions of Native Hawaiians and other Pacific Islanders. We call to all people to demonstrate Hawaiian ideals of sharing at this inaugural special event.


Whether you are Hawaiian or Hawaiian-at-Heart, we welcome you to share in this festival. We stand firm in the traditions and values of our culture as we strive to perpetuate, preserve and protect our heritage. This festival is a great way to connect, reconnect and share our culture with the community at large.


I look forward to meeting you in Chicago.

A hui hou!

Carole Lanialoha Lee-Sumberg

Director of Festival of Aloha
